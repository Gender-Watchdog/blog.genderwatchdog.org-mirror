Legal & Privacy

The Gender Watchdog Blog documents institutional responses to sexual violence in higher education, with focus on exposing institutional capture patterns in Korea through the Dongguk University case. We document how Korean press complicity, QS rankings legitimization, IEQAS certification fraud, and defamation law as legal infrastructure create systematic erasure of violence against international students.
